# Dina Calakovic - B2B Copywriting & Psychology

## Post: The Art of the Hook
**Date:** Recent

"If your first line is boring, the remaining 95% of your LinkedIn post doesn't exist. Period. The sole job of your hook is to make them click 'see more.'

To write irresistible hooks, stop being vague and use psychological triggers:
1. **The Contra-Logic Hook:** State something that goes completely against common knowledge (e.g., 'Why waking up at 4 AM ruined my productivity').
2. **The High Stakes Hook:** Explicitly mention the cost of not reading further (e.g., 'If your SaaS team is doing X, you are wasting $2,000 every single week').
3. **The Data-Backed Story:** Start right in the middle of an action with numbers (e.g., 'It took me 14 months and 42 failures to figure out this one rule').

Do not tease the reader. Give them a reason to lean in immediately."
