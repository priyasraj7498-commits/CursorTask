# Richard van der Blom - LinkedIn Algorithm & Reach Strategy

## Post: Maximizing Your Post's First 60 Minutes
**Date:** Recent

"The first hour after you publish on LinkedIn determines whether your post goes viral or dies. The algorithm uses a 'velocity check' to see how fast people interact with your text.

To maximize your organic reach, focus on these three algorithm rules:
1. **Dwell Time Matters:** The longer someone stays with your post open, the better. Write longer text or structure your layout so they spend time reading it.
2. **Comments Are King:** A comment is worth 12x more than a simple 'Like' in the algorithm. Always reply to comments with a question to keep the conversation going.
3. **Avoid External Links:** Putting a link to an outside website in your main body text reduces your reach by up to 50%. Put your links in the comments section instead to keep people on the platform."
