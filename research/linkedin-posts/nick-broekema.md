# Nick Broekema - LinkedIn Visual Branding Strategy

## Post: The Visual Continuity Rule
**Date:** Recent

"Your written words are only half the battle on LinkedIn. The other half is visual identity. When someone scrolls their feed, they should recognize your post before they even read your name.

Here is how to create instant visual recognition:
1. **Stick to 2 brand colors:** Pick one bright accent color for highlights and one neutral background color. Use them across every graphic or PDF carousel you post.
2. **The 60-30-10 Rule:** 60% of your graphic should be whitespace, 30% should be your clean text, and 10% should be your eye-catching accent element.
3. **Keep fonts consistent:** Never use more than two font families across your assets. One bold font for headings, one clean sans-serif font for readability.

Consistency breeds trust. If your visuals are chaotic, people assume your business strategy is chaotic too."
