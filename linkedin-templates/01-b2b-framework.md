# Template 1: The Counter-Intuitive Framework Post

## Post Copy:
Why working 10 hours a week LESS actually doubled our pipeline.

Most B2B founders think scaling up means scaling hours. It doesn't. It usually just scales burnout. 

Last quarter, we forced our sales team to cut their weekly prospecting hours by 30%. 

The result? Our conversion rate jumped from 4% to 11%.

Here is the exact 3-step framework we used to make it happen (without hiring a single extra rep):

1. Clean the Data First: We stopped targeting broad lists. If a lead hadn't visited our high-intent pricing page in the last 14 days, they were pulled from the queue.
2. The 3-Sentence Rule: We banned long, formal intro emails. If you can't state your value proposition and ask a clear question in 3 sentences, don't send it.
3. Move the Conversation: Stop pitching in the main feed. We used value-driven comments to drive inbound profile clicks.

The secret isn't doing more. It's doing less, with absolute precision.
